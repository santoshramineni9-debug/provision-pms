📚 AR Scenarios - Step by Step Guide

Complete reference for AR calling scenarios with questions, actions, and status codes.

⚠ Points to Check Before Making a Call
  1. Do not miss to check any previous comments in ReLi
  2. Verify claim transaction, if already paid or closed or denied
  3. Check if we already received EOB
  4. See if payer has balance or not
  5. If no transaction notes on ReLi, verify if Claim is sent from ReLi and Gateway
  6. Place Claim reminders for all follow up claims
  7. Ask TFL (Timely Filing Limit) in all calls for all claim status
📞 AR Call Format

Format:

Reason for making a Call Cld "Payor Name"@"Telephone Number" spk to "Rep Name" sd ... Claim# and Ref#. Followed by action and "Initials"


Example:

Per ReLi Claim billed on 052315 & no EOB rcvd. Cld BCBS@18005356890 spk wth Robin sd Claim rcvd on 060115 and need to allow 15 days for processing. Clm#1231211475 & Ref#8752145. Need to allow more time for f/u //RL

⚠ Follow Above notes format

IMPORTANT: AR Call/Work is finished only when required action is taken as per call. Every claim worked needs to have notes accordingly in ReLi.

Every scenario/call must have a reference number - if the claim is processed then the claim number, followed by a request for the duplicate copy of the EOB, through fax/mail.

🔍 Search Scenarios
1 Claim Applied Towards Deductible DED / BILLPT / SBILL ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the processed date and claim #
  2. How much was allowed and applied towards deductible
  3. Was it processed in-network or Out of network
  4. May I know the patient's total in-network deductible and how much has been met
  5. Do you find any other insurance for this patient
  6. Could you fax/mail me a copy of the EOB

✅ Action

  • Bill patient if there is no other insurance listed in system
  • If secondary payer exists, check if we have the EOB. If not, place request for EOB / If we have the EOB, bill secondary payer
Status Code: DED / BILLPT / SBILL
2 Claim Paid to Provider - Paid Through EFT/Direct Deposit CIP / CPNP / CNC ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. Could you tell me the paid date and claim #
  2. What is the allowed amount, contractual adjustment, patient responsibility, paid amount
  3. What is the EFT #/Transaction # and EFT date/Transaction date
  4. Check has been cashed or not and cashed date
  5. Was any procedure code denied
  6. Could you fax me the EOB

✅ Action

  • Follow up according to the TAT
  • If not cashed, follow up accordingly
Status Code: CIP (check <30 days / not issued) | CPNP (>30 days) | CNC (not cashed)
3 Claim Paid to Provider - Paid to Incorrect Address PTWA ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. Could you tell me the paid date and claim #
  2. What is the allowed amount, contractual adjustment, patient responsibility, paid amount
  3. What is the check # and date on check
  4. Is it a bulk check or single check and whom was the check paid to
  5. What is the mailing address of check and if check cashed or not
  6. Why was it sent to incorrect address
  7. Could you give me your fax # to send you a corrected W9
  8. Was any procedure code denied
  9. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Send an email to supervisor to inform client that check # XXX paid on YY to incorrect address still not posted in system
Status Code: PTWA
4 Claim Denied as Inclusive/Incidental COD ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Could you give me the primary procedure?
  3. Would you take the claim for reprocessing if a modifier is given over phone?
  4. Could you give me the fax # mailing address where we can submit corrected claim to
  5. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • If claim was taken for reprocessing, follow up after 30 days / or depends upon processing time
  • Else escalate to coding to rebill with a modifier
Status Code: COD
5 Claim Denied as Duplicate DUP ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Could you give me the Original Denial and original denial date?
  3. Could you give me the fax # mailing address where we can submit corrected claim to
  4. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • If taken for reprocessing, follow up after 30 days / depends on processing time
  • Else escalate to coding or work as per original denial
Status Code: DUP
6 Claim Paid to Patient BILL PT ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the processed date and the claim#?
  2. What is the allowed amount, paid amount, balance bill amount, patient responsibility
  3. Was it processed in network or out of network
  4. Was any procedure code denied
  5. Why was it paid to patient
    1. AOB was not signed
    2. Provider non-par - verify provider details. If provider is par, request to reprocess
  6. Could you Fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Escalate to transfer balance to patient if paid correctly / If have access/approval from client, transfer balance to patient
Status Code: BILL PT
7 Claim Not on File CNOF ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. Do you have any Clearinghouse rejections at your end?
  2. Do you see any other claims for same Provider, same patient, same DOS, different billed amount
  3. May I have the claims mailing address and Payer id?
  4. Is the patient eligible for the DOS
  5. Do you find any other insurance for this patient
  6. May I also have the filing limit for this claim?
  7. May I have the fax # so that I can fax this claim to your attention?
  8. Could you check if the claim was stuck in Payer EDI department, if yes get reason why
  9. Is there a backlog in uploading paper claims in the system?
  10. This is an old DOS - could the claim have been purged/Struck in the system
  11. Could you check if you have received the claim through crossover from Medicare (only for crossover claims)

✅ Action

  • Rebill
Status Code: CNOF
8 Claim in Process CIP ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. Could you tell me the date when you received the claim?
  2. May I have the claim # please?
  3. When the claim was sent for processing/reprocessing?
  4. How long would it take to process this claim? Reason for delay if any
  5. Are there any supporting documents required causing delay
  6. Is the provider eligible for interest payment due to slow processing

✅ Action

  • Follow up according to TAT (Turn Around Time) and place reminder in ReLi
Status Code: CIP
9 Claim Denied for Eligibility PTRESP / BILL PT ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the denial date and claim #
  2. What is the effective and termination date
  3. Do you find this patient enrolled under any other plan
  4. Do you find any other insurance for this patient
  5. Could you fax me the copy of EOB

✅ Action

  • If rep gives corrected id #, document old and new id # and enter corrected id # in system and resubmit
  • For inactive insurance, enter termination date so claim does not get billed again
  • Work on all open claims under MR
  • If no other insurance, bill patient
Status Code: PTRESP / BILL PT
10 Claim Denied for Referral PCP ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Which service requires a referral
  3. Can you give me the PCP's name and telephone number for this patient and DOS
  4. Could you give me the fax number/mailing address where I need to submit the corrected claim
  5. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • If PCP not listed in system, task it to client
  • If PCP is listed in system, add it and refile
Status Code: PCP
11 Claim Denied for Primary EOB - by Primary PRIEOB / COB ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Who is the primary payer for this patient
  3. Could you give me the date from when you are the secondary payer
  4. Could you fax/mail me an EOB please

✅ Action

  • If we have primary insurance details, request to rebill with Primary EOB
  • If we have this as Primary, check eligibility - if no other Insurance found, Bill Pt for correct COB
Status Code: PRIEOB / COB
12 Claim Denied for Primary EOB - by Secondary PRIEOB / COB ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Is XXX (primary in system) the primary payer
  3. If yes, could you give me your fax # so I can fax the primary EOB or give me your mailing address
  4. Could you fax/mail me an EOB please

✅ Action

  • Rebill secondary claim / Bill patient
Status Code: PRIEOB / COB
13 Claim Denied for Prior Authorization NOAUTH ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Which service requires authorization and why?
  3. Can we appeal with Medical Records for retro authorization and what is the time limit?
  4. Could you give me the fax number/mailing address to send retro authorization request?
  5. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • If authorization required because provider is non-participating, escalate to client
  • See if we can bill with PCP and refile - sometimes updating PCP gets it paid
Status Code: NOAUTH
14 Claim Denied as Maximum Benefit Exhausted MAXB ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. What is the maximum benefit that has been met and date when patient met maximum
  3. ONLY FOR MEDICAID: Could you give fax number/mailing address for medical notes
  4. Do you find any other insurance for this patient (Do not ask for Medicaid)
  5. Could you fax a copy of the EOB?

✅ Action

  • Rebill secondary insurance / Bill patient
Status Code: MAXB
15 Claim Denied as Non-Covered Services - Patient NCS ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Could you tell me the services that are not covered under this plan (Dx non covered can be sent to coding)
  3. Can we appeal with medical records? If yes get fax # or mailing address (For Medicaid)
  4. Do you find any other insurance for this patient
  5. Could you fax me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Bill secondary payer / patient
Status Code: NCS
16 Claim Denied as Non-Covered Services - Provider NCS ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the denial date and claim number
  2. Could you tell me the services not covered for this provider
  3. Can we appeal with medical records? If yes, request insurance to reprocess
  4. Could you fax me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Escalate to Coding
Status Code: NCS
17 Claim Denied as Not Medically Necessary NMN ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. May I know why these services are not medically necessary
  3. Could you give me your fax # and mailing address
  4. What is the appealing limit?
  5. Could you fax/mail me an EOB please

✅ Action

  • Escalate to appeal with Medical Records
Status Code: NMN
18 Claim Denied as Primary Paid Maximum PPMAX ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. May I have the allowed amount for this claim?
  3. Can we bill patient for the primary coinsurance?
  4. Could you fax a copy of the EOB?

✅ Action

  • If yes, bill patient - if no, escalate for write off
Status Code: PPMAX
19 Claim Pended/Denied for Additional Info - Provider ADDLINFO ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the processed/Denial date and claim #
  2. Could you tell me the information required for processing the claim
  3. Could you fax me the form that is necessary to be filled by the provider or is it available for download?
  4. What is your fax number/mailing address
  5. Could you fax/mail me a copy of the EOB

✅ Action

  • If claim sent for reprocessing, follow up accordingly
  • If we do not have the information and need to request from practice/client - escalate
Status Code: ADDLINFO
20 Claim Denied as Provider Not Contracted PNC ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. Could you verify provider details - is provider listed as participating under NPI, TAX ID, PIN
  3. Could you give me the effective and termination date of contract (If doctor was par earlier)
  4. What plan does the patient have & provider contract if enrolled with payer
  5. Could you fax/mail me a copy of the EOB

✅ Action

  • Check the contract provider list / escalate for credentials / write-off
Status Code: PNC
21 Claim Pended/Denied for Additional Info - Patient PT-ADDLNFO ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the processed/Denial date of the claim and the claim number
  2. Could you tell me the information required for processing the claim
  3. When was the letter sent to patient
  4. Could you send another request to the patient if sent long time back
  5. What is the call ref #
  6. What is your fax number/mailing address
  7. Could you Fax/mail me a copy of the EOB

✅ Action

  • If denied, check whether we can bill patient
  • If taken for reprocessing, follow up according to TAT
Status Code: PT-ADDLNFO
22 Claim Denied for Exceeding Timely Filing Limit DTF ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. May I have the denial date and claim number
  2. May I have the date when the claim was received
  3. May I have the filing limit for this claim
  4. Do you accept electronic receipts and screen shots as proof of timely filing
  5. What is the appealing limit
  6. Could you give me your fax #/appeals mailing address

✅ Action

  • Check if we have proof in clearing house - if yes, escalate to appeal with POTF (Proof of Timely Filing)
Status Code: DTF
23 Claim Applied Towards Offset OFFSET ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the processed date and claim #
  2. What is the allowed amount, contractual adjustment, patient responsibility, paid amount
  3. How much was the offset amount
  4. Could you give the name and DOS of the account the payment was offset from?
  5. Could you tell me the status of individual claims from where payment was offset? (If one or two encounters)
  6. Was any procedure code denied
  7. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Escalate to posting team to take adjustments accordingly with the EOB
Status Code: OFFSET
24 Claim Sent for Re-Pricing RPRICE ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the processed date and claim #
  2. Date sent for re-pricing
  3. Take re-pricing number and name
  4. Call repricing agency and know claims status and claim#
  5. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Work accordingly
Status Code: RPRICE
25 Claim Applied Towards Capitation CAPITATION ▼

❓ Questions to be Asked

  1. What is the processed date and claim #
  2. Amount which has been processed & applied towards capitation
  3. Could you fax/mail me an EOB

✅ Action

  • Adjust as capitation
Status Code: CAPITATION
